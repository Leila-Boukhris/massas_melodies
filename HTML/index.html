History of Souss Massa Music

The Souss Massa region is home to rich musical traditions shaped by Amazigh (Berber) culture. From the spiritual rhythms of Gnawa, to the celebratory beats of Dakka Houarya, and the poetic chants of Ahwach and Rways, these styles are deeply rooted in community life and storytelling.
