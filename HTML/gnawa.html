Gnawa Music

Gnawa music is a spiritual and rhythmic tradition rooted in the heritage of sub-Saharan Africans brought to Morocco centuries ago. Known for its hypnotic rhythms and ceremonial context, it combines poetry, dance, and the use of traditional instruments like the guembri.

Instruments You Can Try:

  • Guenbri
  • Qraqeb
  • Tbel
