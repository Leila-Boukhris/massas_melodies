Rways Music

Rways is a collective of musicians and poets from the Souss Massa region, known for their traditional performances and storytelling through music. They preserve and perform the oral traditions of the region, combining music with poetry and history.

Instruments You Can Try:

  • Naqos
  • Ribab
  • Tbel
